to flash the mcu with klipper
1. cd ~/klipper
2. make menuconfig
3. make
4. ls /dev/serial/by-id/*
5. sudo service klipper stop
6. make flash FLASH_DEVICE=/dev/serial/by-id/usb-1a86_USB2.0-Serial-if00-port0
7. sudo service klipper start
